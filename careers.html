About CPARK
CPARK is a Digital Performance Marketplace of pay per action programs. We successfully bring together interests of advertisers and publishers by giving them excellent support and strong market expertise to maximize mutual profitability of every deal.
Job Openings
We are looking for the experienced, goal oriented, talented people in our fast-growing team with office in Lviv.
Affiliate Manager
Requirements:

  • At least 1-year experience on the same or relevant field/position (models CPL/CPA)
  • Experience with webmasters and partner programs
  • Strong negotiation and communication skills
  • Proven success in developing strategic partnerships, preferably in an international environment
  • English upper intermediate or higher
  • Attentiveness and persistence in work.

Key responsibilities:

  • Daily work with the existing partners
  • Search of the new affiliate opportunities and partners
  • Check and adjust affiliate's traffic flow
  • Improve the performance of existing digital marketing campaigns
  • Perform set of sales and revenue KPI
  • Help resolve customer service inquiries
  • Monitoring the payment of partners program
  • Weekly reports and performance analysis.
